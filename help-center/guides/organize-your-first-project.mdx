---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new folder">
    Click **New Folder** in your workspace sidebar. Name it after your project section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documents">
    Within the folder, click **New Document**. Give it a title and start writing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag to rearrange">
    Drag folders & docs in the sidebar to reorder as you like.
  </Step>
</Steps>
