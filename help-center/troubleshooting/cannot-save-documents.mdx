description: How to fix save issues in jls.
---

## Symptoms

- Saving a doc fails
- Error: "Unable to save document"

## Quick Fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    ````bash
    ping app.jls.io
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Try reloading your browser window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and log in again">
    Session expiry may cause issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
