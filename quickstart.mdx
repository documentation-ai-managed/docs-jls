## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
- An email address for account verification
- Access to `https://dashboard.example.com`

<Callout kind="info">

jls offers a free tier to get started immediately. Upgrade later for advanced features like custom domains and team collaboration.

</Callout>

## Sign Up and Log In

Follow these steps to create your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and enter your email, password, and name. Verify your email via the confirmation link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and click **Log In**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a Documentation Space

A documentation space organizes your project docs. Create one now.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    In the dashboard, click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select visibility (`public` or `private`), and add a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Space" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create**. Your space is now live at `https://docs.example.com/my-project-docs`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Use descriptive names for spaces. You can create unlimited spaces on the free tier.

</Callout>

## Add Your First Document

Populate your space with content using MDX.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the editor to add MDX. Here's a complete example:
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload">
    Click **Publish**. View live at `/introduction`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Preview">
  ```mdx
  ---
  title: Introduction
  description: Welcome to your docs.
  ---

  ## Overview

  This is your first document.

  <Callout kind="success">Success!</Callout>
  ```
  ```markdown
  # Introduction (Rendered)

  ## Overview

  This is your first document.

  > **Success!** Your docs are live.
  ```
</CodeGroup>
